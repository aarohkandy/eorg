Step 1 of 3 - Enable IMAP in Gmail

Gmail Unified connects using IMAP, a standard email protocol. You need to turn it on in Gmail settings first.

  1. 1Open Gmail
  2. 2Click the gear icon ⚙️ (top right)
  3. 3Click "See all settings"
  4. 4Click the "Forwarding and POP/IMAP" tab
  5. 5Under "IMAP access", select "Enable IMAP"
  6. 6Click "Save Changes"

Step 2 of 3 - Generate an App Password

An App Password is a special code that lets Gmail Unified access your email. It's not your regular Gmail password.

  1. 1Click the button below to open Google App Passwords
  2. 2You may be asked to sign in to your Google account
  3. 3In the "App name" field, type: Gmail Unified
  4. 4Click "Create"
  5. 5Google will show you a 16-character code - copy it
  6. 6Come back here and paste it in the next step
⚠️ The code only appears once. Copy it before closing that page.

App Passwords requires 2-factor authentication to be enabled on your Google account. If the page doesn't work, enable 2FA first at myaccount.google.com/signinoptions/two-step-verification

Step 3 of 3 - Connect Your Account

Paste the 16-character code you just generated.

It may have spaces - that's fine, paste it exactly as shown.
